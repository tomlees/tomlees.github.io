Ollo Portable Table Lamp

Ollo, as a portable table lamp, forms a lighting experience that encourages less energy consumption in the guise of soothing low light intensity.

This soft lighting can be punctuated by moments of high brightness as required.

Adjustable in two gestures, Ollo pivots when twisted to gently increase brightness. When tilted past horizontal Ollo’s top illuminates as a torch.

Ollo understands the importance of light quality, inside and out. Its aluminium body is both durable and timeless. Ollo is an experience in lasting simplicity.
